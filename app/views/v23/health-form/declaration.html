Declaration

By sending the application you agree that:

  • the information you have given on this form is correct and complete
  • you will tell us about changes of circumstances straight away

If the information you give us is wrong or incomplete, or you do not report changes straight away:

  • we may stop or reduce your Personal Independence Payment (PIP)
  • you may be paid too much PIP and have to pay this back
  • you may have to pay a financial penalty
  • we may prosecute you
