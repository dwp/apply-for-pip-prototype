Make sure the person who has asked for their decision to be reconsidered understands that:

  • PIP is assessed on how their condition affects them, not the condition itself

  • the rate of PIP awarded depends on the total points for all of the activities

Explain what will happen next

  • We'll record the details of which parts of the decision they disagree with.
  • There'll be an opportunity to send additional evidence to support the application.
  • Someone will look at all the information again and make another decision.
  • Their points could go up, down or remain the same.
