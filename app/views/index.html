Reasonable adjustments capture - February 2025 - v24

Deprioritised

Some claimants need to attend an in person health assessment. These claimants are sent a letter with their scheduled appointment and asked if they require any reasonable adjustments. Some claimants requiring reasonable adjustments will need to have their appointment rescheduled if the scheduled location cannot accommodate. This costs the department time and money. This is a legal requirement.

Design decisions

  • Task list screen - 'Adjustments at an assessment' as it’s own section.
  • Intro screen - Provide users with a list of examples as to why they might need a reasonable adjustment.
  • Reasonable Adjustments screen - Provide users with checkbox options and ‘Other’ to provide more details.
  • Language requirement screen - This was a requirement from FAS and wasn’t tested with users who would need this. This could be worked into the reasonable adjustments but would need to be test with users who require a translator.
